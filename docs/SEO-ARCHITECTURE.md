## Part 7 — Internal linking rules

- **Every mortgage guide:** 2 related guides, 1 glossary term, 1 calculator.
- **Glossary pages:** 2–3 related guides, 2–3 related glossary terms.
- **Calculators:** 2+ related guides, 1 glossary term (e.g. DTI, amortization).
- **Blog posts:** 1–2 mortgage guides or tools where context fits.
- **Data pages (rates, limits):** Links to relevant guides (e.g. conforming limits → conventional loan guide).

Use `RelatedLinks` or similar component driven by a sitemap/links config.

---

## Part 8 — AI mortgage advisor (/mortgage/find-the-right-loan)

- **Structured flows:** Home purchase → loan types, down payment, affordability. Refinance → break-even, rate vs term. Loan comparison → conventional vs FHA vs VA.
- **Outputs:** Links to guides (e.g. FHA guide, refinance guide), calculators (affordability, refinance analyzer), glossary terms. No product/lender recommendations.
- **Copy:** “Educational guidance only”; suggest reading a guide or trying a calculator.
